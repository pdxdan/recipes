The Protein Colada

It's a cocktail!
It's a meal!
It won't mess up your blood sugar!

Ingredients:
* 1 cup pinapple 
* 1 bananna 
* 1/2 cup coconut meat
* 3 tablespoon hulled hemp seeds
* 1 heaping tablespoon almont butter
* 3 shots of your favorite rum (or more, depending on the type of day you're having)
